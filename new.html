Safe Disposal Guide

Improper disposal of medicines can lead to environmental contamination and accidental poisoning. Expired medicines may also lose potency or become harmful.

  1. Mix medicines with undesirable substance (coffee grounds, kitty litter)
  2. Place mixture in sealed container
  3. Remove personal information from packaging
  4. Dispose in household trash
  5. Check for local take-back programs
  • Opioids and controlled substances
  • Chemotherapy drugs
  • Inhalers and aerosol products
  • Patches and topical medications

Contact your pharmacy or local waste facility for disposal instructions.
